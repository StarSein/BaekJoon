<p>Mr. Kim is a stock market analyst. Recently, he found something interesting while looking at the stock charts of several companies. Most of the stocks that rose for four consecutive days fell the next day. Also, the stock price that fell on the fifth day was often located between the price of the second and third days of the stock price during the four days of the uptrend.
